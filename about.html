The projects page is a good place to see some of my work, but you can also view my resume.

Grew up in Baltimore, Maryland; moved to West Lafayette, Indiana for school; interned in Northport, Michigan and Wichita, Kansas during school; worked in Hunt Valley, Maryland and Fort Worth, Texas after undergrad.
